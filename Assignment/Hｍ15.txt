What is the difference between Web Hosts, Domains, and FTP?


While Web Hosts are to provide spaces and accsess for websites 
which allows customers/organizations to manage their website via the World Wide Web, 
Domains are the identification string that is usually formed with a unique name or 
a company name that appears after the @ sign in email address or after www. in web URL.
FTP is shorten word for the File Transfer Protocol which is for 
the trasnfer of computer files between clients and server on computer network.
